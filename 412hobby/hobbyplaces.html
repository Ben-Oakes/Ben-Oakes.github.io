Place Why it is famous
The Gertrude Ford Center at the University of Mississippi In the 2008, the University of Mississippi hosted the September presidential debate between Barack Obama and John McCain.
Princeton University Princeton University is considered by many to be the origins of debating in a competitive format. In 1769, James Madison founded the first debate union at Princeton University. Princeton University still hosts annual public debate tournaments to this day (or at least they did until covid hit).
The internet! Although not a physical place, online hubs such as zoom have been used to host many debate tournaments during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. Many Universities in 2020 and 2021 opted to hold their annual tournaments online via Zoom and sometimes discord. Specialized websites like Tabbycat are used to distribute round brackets, results, and ballots to competitors in a digital format.
